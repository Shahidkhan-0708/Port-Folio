# CyberGlass Space Design System Guidelines

A highly premium and modern design system designed for a creative tech portfolio.

## Aesthetics & Core Theme
- **Theme**: Deep Space Obsidian (`#080710`) backdrop combined with floating glassmorphic container cards (`#120f26` at `0.6` opacity) that blur whatever is behind them.
- **Accents**: 
  - Electric Cyber Violet (`#8a2be2`) for dominant brand gradients.
  - Neon Cyan (`#00f5ff`) for interactive elements, highlights, and secondary states.
  - Neon Mint (`#00ff87`) for success messages or indicators.
- **Animations**:
  - Linear neon border flow effects.
  - Pulsating blur circles in the background (purple and cyan) that drift gently.
  - Glass-tilt transitions on grid cards.

## Typographical Hierarchy
- Headers use **Syne** (a modern, wide, geometric display font) with bold weights and tight tracking for an ultra-contemporary editorial look.
- Body text utilizes **Plus Jakarta Sans** for maximum readability, modern curves, and clean spacing.
